Cooper Athletics

The first step

This project was created as part of my Website Design course at Northwest University, using Weebly as the content management system. I designed and produced all site elements, including logos, images, videos, blog posts, layout, and navigation, ensuring a cohesive and user-friendly experience.

The concept for the site was to support parents and volunteer coaches who are new to youth sports. While many children are eager to explore new activities, parents and coaches often feel unprepared or unsure where to begin. This site was designed to bridge that gap by offering “how-to” videos, coaching tips, and workout plans to help guide them in preparing for their chosen sport.

Although the current version includes drills and activities as placeholders for the “how-to” videos, it demonstrates my ability to structure content, develop branded visuals, and design a clear navigation flow to meet a specific audience’s needs. The project also includes a promotional ad, which added a creative dimension to the overall design!
