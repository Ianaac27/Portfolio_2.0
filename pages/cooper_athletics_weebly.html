Cooper Athletics

The first step

Here is a website I had created for my Website Design course at Northwest University. We utilized Weebly as our CMS to create the site. I had designed and created all content on the sight including logos, images, videos, blogs, layout, and website navigation.

Many young kids begin to become interested in trying new sports, bringing many parents to the position of looking to be involved whether it is at home or volunteering to coach a team. However, the situation often arises where the parent or coach is unprepared, and even unsure on where to start. This website's intention is to provide this support by providing a variety of "how-to" videos, coaching tips, and workout plans that can be utilized for preparation in their desired sport. At this point, there are no actual "how-to" videos. Instead, a variety of drills are listed as placeholders for the purpose of this project.
