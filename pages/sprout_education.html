Sprout Education

Learning with AI

Sprout Education is an AI-powered learning companion designed to help students connect what they learn in the classroom to real-world application. Instead of using AI to replace learning, Sprout supports students ethically by guiding them through reflective conversations that build understanding, strengthen retention, and promote deeper thinking.

The app also teaches students how to create and follow SMART goals, acting as both a study partner and personal learning coach. Through interactive sessions, Sprout helps learners track progress, apply knowledge meaningfully, and stay focused on their academic and personal growth.

Sprout Education was built and hosted using Bolt AI, which allowed for rapid development and iteration. The portfolio links include the live app and documentation explaining its features and design. Future enhancements and updates are planned as the project continues to improve.
