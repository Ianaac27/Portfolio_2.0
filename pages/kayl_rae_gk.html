Everything Goalkeeping!

I started a YouTube channel inspired by my daughter’s love for goalkeeping and her dream of building a presence in this space. I handle every part of the channel’s production, from creating custom logos and eye-catching thumbnails to editing video and audio and producing content that connects with viewers.

To expand her online presence, I’m also building a companion website that will showcase highlight reels, her soccer schedule, live game links, an “About Me” page, and a photo gallery. I designed a mock site and wireframe in Figma to plan the layout, functionality, and overall user experience.

This project combines creativity with technical skills in branding, multimedia production, and web design while supporting my daughter’s vision.
