Digital Learning Virtual Classroom

Love to learn

I designed and developed a virtual classroom tailored for K-5 students during my tenure as a Digital Learning Specialist at Monroe School District. This virtual learning environment is built on the Canvas Learning Management System (LMS) and incorporates Google Slides for content updates. The primary objective of this virtual classroom is to offer students a versatile digital resource accessible both during class and from the comfort of their homes. Within this platform, students have access to a wealth of digital learning materials spanning various subjects, including but not limited to digital citizenship, coding, typing, algorithms, and more, fostering an enriched learning experience.
