Who I am

An educator and more

I’ve spent over a decade working in education, wearing many hats. I have supported technology needs, managed projects, troubleshooted issues, developed courses, written technical documentation, and taught and trained students of all ages. What drives me most is being at the intersection of education and technology, finding creative ways to make learning more engaging and work more efficient.

I am currently pursuing an M.Ed in Digital Education Leadership at Seattle Pacific University. This program has given me the opportunity to take on exciting projects that combine emerging technologies like AI with effective instructional design. These experiences, along with my professional background, have strengthened my skills in course design, asynchronous learning, technical writing, and LMS management. I am eager to apply these skills to help educators and students make the most of the tools available to them.

As a former teacher, I have always aimed to inspire learners to think creatively, solve problems, and leverage the resources around them. That philosophy carries over into my current work, where I enjoy helping people adopt new technologies in ways that are meaningful and practical. My experience in project management, training, and content development allows me to create learning experiences that are both engaging and effective.

Outside of work, I balance life with fitness, sports, creating fun content, and spending time with my family. I bring that same energy and curiosity to my professional work, always looking for ways to innovate and improve learning experiences. Please take a look at some of my work below!
